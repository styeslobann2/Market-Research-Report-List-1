<p><p>The Outdoor Air Quality Monitoring System market encompasses various types of monitoring methods. Active or continuous monitoring involves real-time data collection using automated sensors, providing immediate insights into air quality. Passive monitoring utilizes absorbent materials to capture pollutants over time, requiring laboratory analysis. Intermittent monitoring involves periodic data collection, balancing detail and resource use. Manual monitoring relies on human intervention for sampling and analysis. Stack monitoring focuses specifically on emissions from industrial stacks, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
