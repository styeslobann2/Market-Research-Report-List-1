<p><strong>The Paralleling Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed</li><li>Air-Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Paralleling Reactor market can be categorized into two primary types: oil-immersed and air-core reactors. Oil-immersed reactors utilize oil for insulation and cooling, offering enhanced efficiency and thermal management, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. Conversely, air-core reactors rely on air as an insulating medium, promoting easier maintenance and lower costs, though they may have limitations in handling high power levels. Both types serve to improve power quality, managing reactive power and stabilizing voltage in electrical systems.</p></p>
